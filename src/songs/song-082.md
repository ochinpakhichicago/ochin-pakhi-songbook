[Refrain]
T: Jedin hindu, musliman, bouddh, khristan
B: যেদিন হিন্দু মুসলমান বৌদ্ধ খ্রিষ্টান
E: On the day when Hindu, Muslim, Buddhist, Christian

T: Jati-gotro nahi robe
B: জাতি গোত্র নাহি রবে
E: No caste or lineage will remain

T: Emon manob samaj kobe go
B: এমন মানব সমাজ কবে গো
E: When will such a human society come to be?

T: Kobe go srijon hobe?
B: কবে গো সৃজন হবে?
E: When will it be created?

[Verse 1]
T: Shunaye lobhero buli
B: শুনায়ে লোভের বুলি
E: Speaking words of temptation

T: Nebe na keu kaNdher jhuli
B: নেবে না কেউ কাঁধের ঝুলি
E: No one will take another's burden on their shoulder

T: Etor atorafo boli
B: ইতর অতরাফ বলি
E: Calling others lowly and outcast

T: Dure thele nahi dibe
B: দূরে ঠেলে নাহি দিবে
E: Will not push them away

[Verse 2]
T: Amir-fakir hoye ekthai
B: আমির ফকির হয়ে একঠাই
E: The wealthy and the poor in one place

T: Sobar paona pabe sobai
B: সবার পাওনা পাবে সবাই
E: Everyone will receive what is rightfully theirs

T: Ashraf boliya rehai
B: আশরাফ বলিয়া রেহাই
E: Calling oneself noble (ashraf)

T: Bhabe keho nahi pabe
B: ভাবে কেহ নাহি পাবে
E: No one will find escape in that thought

[Verse 3]
T: Dhormo kulo gotro jatir
B: ধর্ম কুল গোত্র জাতির
E: Of religion, clan, lineage, and caste

T: Tulbe na keu kono jigir
B: তুলবে না কেউ কোনো জিগির
E: No one will raise any dispute or claim

T: KeNde bole lalon fakir
B: কেঁদে বলে লালন ফকির
E: Weeping, Lalon Fakir says

T: Ke more dekhaye debe
B: কে মোরে দেখায়ে দেবে
E: Who will show me the way?

[Glossary]
jati-gotro | জাতি গোত্র | caste and lineage — the two markers of social hierarchy
lobher buli | লোভের বুলি | words of greed / temptation
jhuli | ঝুলি | bag, bundle — carried on the shoulder; here a metaphor for another's burden
etor | ইতর | lowly, contemptible — a derogatory term applied to lower castes
atorafo | অতরাফ | outcast — from the Arabic "atraf," those considered beyond social pale
amir | আমির | the wealthy, a ruler
fakir | ফকির | wandering spiritual mendicant; the poor and dispossessed
ashraf | আশরাফ | the noble — Arabic term for those claiming descent from the Prophet
jigir | জিগির | dispute, claim, chanting — raising a cry of superiority

[Arrangement]
Tune to Algoza.
Sam alap to start then subho slow. Sindhi break in between. Then Dotara brings in rhythm. Again Jedin from Subho.
Nate enters with the first interlude.
Molly: Dotted quarter dotted quarter quarter, whole note rest. And 1 and 3 and 1 and 3 for faster groove.
Groove goes into double time for itor atraf, hits on Dure thele.
(bhajan theka) Interludes: bansuri, dotara, violin.
Sam: play low D after Ke more dekhaye debe on Chorus.
Then algoza breakdown after ke more dekhay debe. Subho joins algoza when Nate lands on and holds Pa. Nate continues to jam as Subho improvises melody.
Coming back to Emon manob Subho leads. Emon 3X, last time kobe P D N S hits to end rhythm and Subho and Nate end slow.
