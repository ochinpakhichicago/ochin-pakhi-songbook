[Refrain]
T: Se ki amar kobar kotha
B: সে কি আমার কবার কথা
E: Is this something I can even speak of?

T: Apon bege aponi mori
B: আপন বেগে আপনি মরি
E: I die by my own momentum

T: (O mon) Gaur eshe hride bose
B: (ও মন) গৌর এসে হৃদে বসে
E: (O my heart) Gaur came and sat in my heart

T: Korlo amar monchuri
B: করল আমার মনচুরি
E: And stole my mind away

[Verse 1]
T: Kiba gaur rup lampote
B: কিবা গৌর রূপ লম্পটে
E: What a beauty-obsessed form Gaur has!

T: Dhairyer duri dey go kete
B: ধৈর্যের দুরি দেয় গো কেটে
E: He cuts through the rope of my patience

T: Lojja bhoy sob jay go chute
B: লজ্জা ভয় সব যায় গো ছুটে
E: All shame and fear go running away

T: Jokhon ai rup mone kori
B: যখন ঐ রূপ মনে করি
E: Whenever I think of that form

[Verse 2]
T: Ghumer ghore dekhlam jare
B: ঘুমের ঘরে দেখলাম যারে
E: The one I saw in the room of sleep (in dreams)

T: Chetan hoye paine tare
B: চেতন হয়ে পাই নে তারে
E: When awake, I cannot find him

T: Lukaile kon shohore
B: লুকাইলে কোন শহরে
E: In which city has he hidden himself?

T: Nob roser rasbihari
B: নব রসের রসবিহারী
E: The one who revels in the nine rasas (essences of love)

[Verse 3]
T: Meghe jemon chataker e
B: মেঘে যেমন চাতকের এ
E: Like the chatak bird with the rain cloud

T: Dekha diye fanke fere
B: দেখা দিয়ে ফাঁকে ফেরে
E: He shows himself then slips away

T: Lalon bole tai amare
B: লালন বলে তাই আমারে
E: Lalon says, that is why with me

T: Korlo gaur barabari
B: করল গৌর বারবারী
E: Gaur has done this again and again

[Glossary]
kobar | কবার | to speak of, to express
bege | বেগে | with speed, with momentum
mori | মরি | I die
gaur | গৌর | the fair one — the divine beloved
hride | হৃদে | in the heart
monchuri | মনচুরি | mind-theft, stealing the mind
rup | রূপ | form, beauty, appearance
lampote | লম্পটে | one addicted to beauty, beauty-obsessed
dhairyer | ধৈর্যের | of patience
duri | দুরি | rope, cord
lojja | লজ্জা | shame, modesty
bhoy | ভয় | fear
ghumer ghore | ঘুমের ঘরে | in the room of sleep — in dreams
chetan | চেতন | awake, conscious
lukaile | লুকাইলে | has hidden (himself)
nob ros | নব রস | nine rasas — the essences/flavors of love in devotional poetry
rasbihari | রসবিহারী | one who revels in rasa, the playful divine
chatak | চাতক | a mythical bird that only drinks falling rainwater — symbol of intense longing
meghe | মেঘে | in the cloud
fanke | ফাঁকে | in the gap, slipping away
barabari | বারবারী | again and again, repeatedly

[Arrangement]
Subho solo with light dotara plucking, then he slips into rhythm.
Nate Khol in on "Gaur eshe"
Molly along on ektara 1st time
Chorus "se ki" is repeated by everyone but Subho 2nd time
Dotara 2nd time
Sam or Tzip? 1st interlude, Mehtab enters
Dotara 2nd interlude
Sam 3rd interlude
Building through "Gaur eshe" then fade out for the end 3× "se ki"
Listen for the swell and drop — hold back volume, let it simmer.
